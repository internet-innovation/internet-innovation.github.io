**Kyle MacMillan** is a JD/PhD Candidate at the University of Chicago studying issues at the intersection of law and technology. He is a member of the NOISE Lab and is broadly interested in internet measurement, networks, and their applications in law and policy. He is advised by Nick Feamster.
