**Alexis Schrubbe, PhD** was the Director of the Internet Innovation Initiative at the University of Chicago. She led a cross-disciplinary research and policy team focused on advancing digital infrastructure, accessibility, and public interest technology. At UChicago, she led the development of large-scale data platforms that support broadband mapping, funding distribution, and public accountability.
